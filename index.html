Welcome

My name is Madelyn but I prefer to go by Maddie. I am a dancer with many other hobbies, including photography of course! I photograph dance to offer my audience an alternate view on the art. Creating shapes with the body and expressing passion through the uniqueness of dance will always be soemthing I love. I find dance so magical, hence "Maddie J's Magical Moments." I work to capture the magic and beauty of dance. I discovered my love for dance over a decade ago, and my love for photographing it about five years later. I hope you enjoy my work!

-me-



My interest in dance photography began a very long time ago. Although, I've just recently taken it on as a primary practice. Growing up in the dance world, I did a ton of photoshoots. On my Ipad, I loved to take dance pictures of myself. From here, my passion and skill continued to grow; and I hope this trend continues on.

These are dance photos that I took of my friends on my Nikon Coolpix that I recieved as a Christmas gift. I was around 10 years old when I took these. I loved this camera, and still have it!
